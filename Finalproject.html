Final Project

This series focuses on objects found in my backyard. Things that are considered imperfect. I was interested in giving these objects more attention by adding glitches. I wanted to highlight the imperfections rather than hide them. The distortions draw the viewer’s eye and turn the flaws into something intentional and visually appealing. I wanted to challenge what we consider “beautiful” and show that even flawed objects can hold visual and conceptual value.
