Glitch Art

I chose to glitch photos of a cartoon I used to watch as a kid named Ni Hao Kai Lan. The idea was to express the feeling of growing up and slowly losing memories of your favorite cartoon. It becomes a blur and is no longer as bright and colorful as you once saw it.
