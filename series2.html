Self Portrait

Religon has always been a part of my life since i was raised in a catholic chruch. In this photo, I placed a rosary across my arm and hand in a specific way. I wanted the cross and the beads to run along my arm, almost like veins. My idea was to show how being raised catholic feels like something that is engraved in me or running through me. It's something that has always been present in my life since I was little.
